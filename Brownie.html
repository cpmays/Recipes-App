Fudgy Oreo Brownie

Easy Oreo brownies recipe from scratch that are super fudgy and loaded with chocolate. They are ready in less than 1 hour and make a great dessert or snack.



  • Prep Time: 10 Minutes

  • Cook Time: 30 Minutes

  • Total Time: 40 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter +2 tbsp butter
  • 1.5 cups Granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup Cocoa powder
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup All-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup Semisweet Chocolate chips
  • 12 Roughly chopped Oreos

Directions

  1. In a microwave-safe mixing bowl, add butter, sugar, cocoa powder and heat in microwave at 30 second increments (mixing between each round) until the butter has fully melted and you have a smooth mixture.
  2. Add eggs, vanilla extract and mix again.
  3. Add flour, chocolate chips and mix until just combined. Don't over-mix! Note: Some or all of the chocolate chips might melt, depending on how warm your mixture is. Don't worry about that!
  4. Pour batter in a square pan lined with aluminum foil and greased with butter or oil (Dimensions: 8x8). Spread it evenly with a spatula.
  5. Top it off with chopped Oreos.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes or until an inserted toothpick comes out with a few sticky crumbs. If there are no sticky crumbs, you have over-baked the brownies.
  7. Allow the brownies to fully cool down before cutting slices. Enjoy!
